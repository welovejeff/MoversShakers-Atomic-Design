Body Text (Inter)

Body Large (18px): We use yellow as our strong brand color to excite and draw attention to big moments. While the use of our brand color is important for recognition, Yellow should always be balanced by clean white space.

Body Regular (16px): These descriptive words can describe what is going on in a scene. These can be used to give more insight into the video in social, in a pitch deck, or in a still on the website.

Body Small (14px): Supporting text and captions. Used for metadata, timestamps, and secondary information that doesn't require primary attention.

Body XS (12px): Fine print, legal text, and micro-copy. Use sparingly and ensure adequate contrast.
