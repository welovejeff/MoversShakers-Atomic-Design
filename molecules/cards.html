Cards

Content containers with various layouts and styles for displaying information.

Basic Variants

Basic Card

A simple card component with padding, border, and the signature brand shadow.

Yellow Shadow

Same card structure but with the brand yellow accent shadow for emphasis.

Outlined Card

A subtle card variant with lighter border, great for dense layouts.

Filled Card

No border, just a subtle background fill. Clean and minimal.

Dark Card

Inverted colors for high contrast sections or dark themes.

Highlighted Card

Full yellow background for maximum attention and call-to-action areas.
