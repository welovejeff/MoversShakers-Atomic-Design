Placement Guidelines

✓ Do

  • • Appear at cursor position
  • • Flip to stay within viewport
  • • Show relevant options only
  • • Include keyboard shortcuts
  • • Group related actions

✗ Don't

  • • Show more than 10 items without grouping
  • • Nest more than 2 levels deep
  • • Include primary actions (use buttons)
  • • Auto-dismiss on hover out
